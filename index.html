MAJEM CLOTHING AND TEXTILES

Executive Summary

Majem Clothing is a bespoke luxury tailoring business that combines creativity, craftsmanship, and sustainability. Founded in 2008 by Alamin Hafiz Maje, a graduate of Kano University of Science and Technology (B.Eng Civil), the company has grown from a small rented shop with manual sewing machines to a recognized brand with industrial production capabilities and an international customer base.

Majem Clothing plans to expand its product offering by launching a men’s woven boxers line under the brand “MAJEMWEAR” in 2025-2026. This product launch will pave the way for future lines including men’s underwear, three-quarter trousers, and Arabian men's gowns branded as “MajemJallab”. The company also prides itself on its commitment to sustainable practices, having transitioned to solar energy to power operations.

Company History

Majem Clothing began with three manual sewing machines in a rented shop. Guided by experienced tailors, the founder and his friends gained valuable skills and steadily upgraded equipment, moving to industrial machines by 2019. The business was officially branded as “Majem Clothing” with active social media presence and registered as a company in 2020.

In 2022, the company expanded to a larger workshop, doubled production capacity, and added new industrial machines. The team has grown, and the business now operates using solar power, reducing environmental impact and operational costs. Majem Clothing serves a diverse clientele, including government officials and international customers across Europe, Asia, and the Americas.
